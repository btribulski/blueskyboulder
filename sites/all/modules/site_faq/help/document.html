Create a Document

  • Select Create Content - Document
  • Enter the Title
  • Click on Menu Settings to show options:
    • Enter a link title
  • Enter a short description in Body
  • Leave Committees set to public
  • Scroll down to File attachments and click to show options
    • Attach new file if there is a MS Word or PDF document you would like to attach,
  • URL Path settings - Leave automatic alias checked
  • Click Save at bottom of page

Now update The Meeting Minutes Main Page

I have added a Meeting Minutes "Main" Page (currently for members only) at:
http://www.domainnamereplacement.com/content/meeting-minutes

  • Copy the url of the "document" created in the steps above (should be in your browser after you click save) or use the menu link created to get to the document. Highlight the url in your browser and press ctrl+c or right click mouse - copy
  • Navigate to http://www.domainnamereplacement.com/content/meeting-minutes in your Browser
  • Click Edit at the top of the page
  • In the body area Highlight the Minutes from the Last Meeting text
  • Click on the Inset/Edit Link in the Body Area's formatting bars (looks like a chain link 2nd row 1st button on left)
  • In the pop-up window in the URL box: Paste the url of the document created in the steps above press ctrl+v or right click mouse - paste - Click OK
  • Scroll down to bottom of page and Click Save
